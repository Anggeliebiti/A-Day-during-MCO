My Life During MCO

By Anggelie Biti

Hey there! I'am Anggelie. Firstly, let me tell you about MCO. MCO stands for Movement Control Order as a preventive measure towards the Coronavirus.
Therefore, we could not go anywhere and must stay at home for our own safety and health.

These are the few activities that I have done during this MCO.

Workout

I usually go jog when I had a free time. Since I cannot go out, I decided to do workout at home.

Makeup

Makeup is one of my hobbies. With the free time that I had, I learned more about makeup tutorial in youtube and pinterest.

Baking

I love to bake too. So I try to find some recipe that are easy to bake.

Watching movies and Netflix

Every night, I will watched a movie or watched one of my favourite series in Netflix.

Writing A Journal

During this MCO, I also started to write in my journal again.
